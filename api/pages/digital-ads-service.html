Workflow
  • 1 Initial consultation
    we will schedule an initial consultation to discuss project details and reach a mutual agreement on the scope of work. We will thoroughly understand the client's requirements and provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, timelines and costs.
  • 2 Initial payment
    Upon agreement, the client will be required to make an initial payment of 50% of the total project fee as a show of commitment. This payment serves as an indication of the client's commitment to the project and will be used to cover the costs of development.
  • 3 Development
    Our team of developers will commence work on the project immediately after receipt of payment. The developers will use the latest tools and technologies to deliver the project on time and to the highest standards.
  • 4 Review and feedback
    Upon completion of the project, it will be submitted for client review and feedback. The client will have the opportunity to review the project and provide feedback on any revisions or changes they would like to see.
  • 5 Revisions
    Any necessary revisions will be made in accordance with the client's feedback. Our developers will work with the client to ensure that the project meets their expectations and requirements.
  • 6 Final submission
    Upon completion of revisions, the final project will be submitted for the client's approval. The client will have the opportunity to review the final project and provide their final approval.
  • 7 Final payment
    he remaining 50% of the project fee will be payable before the final submission of the project. This payment serves as a final confirmation of the client's acceptance of the project and will be used to cover the costs of final revisions and delivery.
  • 8 Delivery and support
    After receipt of final payment, the project will be delivered to the client. The project will be delivered in the desired format and with all necessary documentation and support. Our team will be available for any post-delivery support and assistance.
